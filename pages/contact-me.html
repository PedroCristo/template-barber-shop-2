CANCELLATION & LATE POLICIES

Cancellations are accepted 24 hours in advance of your scheduled appointment.

There is a 15 minute grace period for clients that are running late. Any time beyond the 15 minutes will forfeit the appointment and it will have to be rescheduled. Appointments are scheduled 45 minutes apart.
