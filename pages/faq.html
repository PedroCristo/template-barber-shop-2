FAQ

Where are you located?

We’re located right in the heart of Midtown East Manhattan between Bryant Park to the West and Grand Central Station right across the street. Our address is 315 Madison Ave Suite 211. The entrance to the building is on 42nd street next to Chick-fil-A.

What is the closest subway station and bus stop to your location?

Subway: Grand Central Station (4, 5, 6, & 7 Lines) or 42 St-Bryant Park Station (B, D, F, & M Lines)

Bus: M1, M2, M3, M4, Q32, M42, M101, M102, M103, BXM7, BXM8, BXM10, BXM18, QM21, SIM6, SIM11, 0028, Shortline Hudson

Are you available for off-site event hair services?

Absolutely, we are available for any events where there will be multiple men getting their hair done such as for Weddings for the Groom, Groomsman and any other family events. Call or Contact Us for more details and pricing.

Do you offer barber-style clipper haircuts?

I’m sorry but at this time we do not offer clipper haircuts. All of our haircuts offered are achieved using scissor over comb styling technique for a more textured look.

How do I book online with a stylist?

You can either download the Vagaro app on your Android or iPhone. Or you can simply follow this link to book via a your web browser on your computer or mobile device.

Do I have to use the Vagaro App to book online?

While most of our clients do book online via Vagaro, there are a few that still like to book with us via Phone or through the Contact Us page of my website. If you don’t get through via phone call, please do leave a voice message and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible. Once the booking has been placed, please be sure to receive a confirmation from us to ensure that you are locked into our schedule.

How do I book online with a stylist?

As of right now we do not except walk-ins without an appointment but the best way to see if anyone is available last minute is to give us a ring or text @ 784-9845798. If we don’t pick up, please leave a voice message and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
